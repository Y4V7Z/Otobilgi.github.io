Bu gördüğünüz italyan boğası yani kendi adıyla Lamborghini Aventador LP750-4 SV (SuperVeloce), Lamborghini'nin Aventador serisinin yüksek performanslı bir versiyonudur.
2015 yılında tanıtılmıştır ve sınırlı sayıda üretilmiştir.

Bu motor, Lamborghini'nin motor sporları tecrübesinden ilham alınarak tasarlanmış olup, hem hız hem de ses performansıyla öne çıkar.

Engine Features

Engine Type: Aspirated V12
Displacement: 6.5 liters (6,498 cc)
Power: 750 horsepower (hp) @ 8,400 rpm
Torque: 690 Nm @ 5,500 rpm
Maximum RPM: 8,500 rpm
Fuel System: Multi-point injection system
Cooling: Liquid cooling system

Performance

0-100 km/h Acceleration: 2.8 seconds
Maximum Speed: 350 km/h (approx.)
Weight: 1,525 kg (approx.)
4WD System: 4-wheel drive system that transmits high torque to the road
Transmission: 7-speed ISR (Independent Shifting Rods) automatic transmission

Power Transmission

Transmission: 7-speed ISR (Independent Shifting Rod) automatic manual
Drive System: All-wheel drive (AWD)

Key Features of the Motor

Lightweight Material Usage: Weight reduction is achieved through the use of carbon fiber and aluminum in the engine and other components.
Titanium Exhaust System: Features a lighter and performance-oriented exhaust design.
High Rev Capacity: The high rev limits offered by the naturally aspirated engine provide a pure and aggressive driving experience.
